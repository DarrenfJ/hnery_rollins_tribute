some words about the man.machine

"hit the street and pursued the minimum wage jobs.. that was me in the late 70's"

"I had... a little tiny life with my minimum wage job.. which I liked"

"then i got a lucky break.. at that time i was very frustrated... this is going to be a very tough life.."

"a life of.. a lot of work.. taking it from other people.. and having very little to show for it..."

"and then. Black Flag. the famous band... I jumped on stage and sang with them"

"we're holding auditions, you want a crack at this? I looked at the ice cream scoop in my hand.. and my future.. so I took a train up there"

"ok you're in.. you're the singer in Black Flag.. I said yes to everything.. worked like a crazy man"

"I don't have talent. I have tenacity. I have discipline. I have Focus. I know, without any delusion, where I come from & where I can go back to."

timeline: or, how he got in the van and made punk rawk

  • born Henry Lawrence Garfield; February 13, 1961
  • Late 70's
  • discovers Punk Rock with friend Ian MacKaye (Minor Threat, Fugazi)
  • of course, through the Ramones debut album
  • Roadie for many local Washington DC bands
  • Sometimes singer for some of them
  • Sometimes he would sing with Bad Brains
  • In short lived band State of Alert
  • Early 80's
  • Manager of a Hagen Das store
  • 1981 Black Flag!!
  • all kinds of other stuff
  • Rollins band
  • Lollapallooza tour
  • spoken word stuff
  • own publishing house
  • own talk show
  • movies
  • but for me: he was in Black flag!

being a kid in a small town and discovering Hank

I suppose you hadda be a kid, in a small town in Northern Ontario, to get why discovering the music of Hank was such a thing. You had to be there at that time to get it.

I mean you have this music which is all about intensity and chaos, coupled with someone who is always saying 'you just have to get up and do it'

'get focussed' 'do your thing' 'do it' 'and just do it fully'

Of course this isn't a quote.. it's how I interpretted what he was saying.

And if you were in that small town wondering what it all means and how you fit into a world you just didn't fit in to (you know the whole round peg in a square hole thang) finding Hank was like 'YES!'
